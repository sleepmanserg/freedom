Start Investing in IPOs With Just $2,000

An Initial Public Offering (IPO) refers to the process through which a private company sells its shares to the public for the first time and starts to trade on stock exchanges like Nasdaq. IPOs are often associated with large companies or wealthy private investors who can spend millions of dollars to secure a worthy deal. At Freedom24, we aim to provide easy and affordable IPO access to anyone who would like to become a shareholder. You can participate in the rosiest IPOs with a starting capital of just $2,000

How It Works

1
Create An Account
Register with Freedom24 to start getting analytics, professional advice on relevant matters, and recommendations on the best IPOs from our analysts. You’ll have a few days to review the analytics and decide on your participation
2
Submit Application For IPO Participation
Once you decide to participate in a company's IPO, you need to submit an application indicating your interest in the Issuer's shares and the amount you want to buy. We'll collect all confirmed applications and form a single one that will be sent to the underwriter
3
IPO Share Allocation
You will get a notification as soon as the IPO shares have been allocated to you. The notification will detail the number of shares allocated and the total price. Afterward, you just wait till the stock hits the market
4
Price Fluctuation
Usually, the price for the stocks will start to change as soon as it hits the exchange. The price increase is often caused by higher demand from traders who didn't participate in the IPO. However, there is a 93-days lock-up period for the shares purchased during the IPO
5
Sell or Keep Your Stocks
After the lock-up period, you can now decide whether to leave the stocks in your portfolio or cash in on them. Investing and managing your wealth with IPOs has never been easier; join Freedom24 to get started now!
